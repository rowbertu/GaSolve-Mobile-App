Frequently Asked Questions
reCAPTCHA
Q1
May we know the amount of school fees for SY 2025-2026
⌄
Please click or tap the link for the updated school fees for SY 2025-2026. The fees are updated for the Hyflex (Hybrid-Flexible) Learning and Teaching Modality. You will also be given a hard copy of this for your reference.
Q2
What are the requirements for admission/enrollment for the NEW students?
⌄
New students are required to submit the following documents: birth certificate (PSA copy), report card from previous school, good moral certificate, medical records, and 2x2 ID photos. Additional requirements may vary depending on the grade level.
Q3
What are the details of online payment for school fees?
⌄
We accept online payments through various channels including bank transfers, GCash, and credit/debit cards. Payment instructions and account details will be provided upon enrollment. A confirmation receipt will be issued for all online transactions.
Q4
What are the processes and Documents needed for the Enrollment?
⌄
The enrollment process includes: filling out the application form, submitting required documents, payment of enrollment fees, and attending the orientation. Documents needed include completed enrollment form, assessment form, and all required certificates.
Q5
What is the school calendar for SY 2025-2026?
⌄
The school year 2025-2026 will follow the DepEd approved calendar. Classes will begin in August 2025 and end in May 2026. Specific dates for holidays, breaks, and examination periods will be provided during enrollment.
Q6
What scholarships are available for students?
⌄
We offer various scholarship programs including academic scholarships, athletic scholarships, and financial assistance programs. Eligibility criteria and application procedures are available at the school registrar's office.
Q7
What are the school uniform requirements?
⌄
School uniforms are required for all students. The prescribed uniform includes the school polo shirt, pants/skirt, and school shoes. PE uniforms are also required for physical education classes. Uniforms can be purchased from our authorized suppliers.
Q8
What extracurricular activities are offered?
⌄
We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports teams, music and arts programs, academic clubs, and community service organizations. Students are encouraged to participate to develop their talents and interests.
Q9
What is the student-to-teacher ratio?
⌄
We maintain a favorable student-to-teacher ratio to ensure personalized attention. Our average class size is 25-30 students, allowing teachers to provide quality instruction and individual support to each student.
Q10
Are there transportation services available?
⌄
Yes, we provide school bus transportation services covering various routes in Metro Manila. Transportation fees are separate from tuition. Route details and schedules are available at the school office.
Q11
What are the school hours?
⌄
Regular school hours are from 7:30 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday through Friday. Preschool and kindergarten have slightly different schedules. Extended hours and after-school programs are also available.
Q12
Does the school have a cafeteria?
⌄
Yes, we have a school cafeteria that offers nutritious meals and snacks. Students can purchase food or bring packed lunches. We maintain high standards of food safety and nutrition in all our cafeteria offerings.
Q13
How can parents stay updated on their child's progress?
⌄
Parents can access their child's grades, attendance, and progress through our online portal. We also conduct regular parent-teacher conferences, send progress reports, and maintain open communication channels via email and phone.
